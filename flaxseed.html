  • Storage: Keep in a cool, dark place; refrigeration after opening is recommended for best freshness.

Benefits & Uses

Food & Kitchen

  • Use as a finishing oil over salads, steamed vegetables or cooked grains.
  • Blend a spoon into smoothies, dips or chutneys for a gentle nutty note.
  • Best used without high heat; avoid deep frying or prolonged high-temperature cooking.

Skin & Hair Care

  • Apply a few drops on slightly damp skin as a simple face or body oil.
  • Massage into scalp and hair lengths before shampooing as part of your oiling routine.
  • Always patch test first and avoid using on broken or irritated skin.

How to Use

For Skin

Place a few drops on clean palms, spread over face or body and massage gently until absorbed; wipe or wash off if you prefer a lighter feel.

For Hair
